From Reservation to Arrival

  1. Create your account

    Use email sign in and verify within 10 minutes.

  2. Select date and slot

    Peak days display limited stock indicators.

  3. Confirm and pay

    Credit card only. A receipt is emailed instantly.

  4. Arrive and scan

    Show QR on mobile. An attendant will guide you.

What if I am late

Grace period is 10 minutes. After that, requeue with staff assistance.
